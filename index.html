About Me
Hi there! Welcome to Jiawen's homepage.

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Texas A&M University, advised by Prof. Jeyavijayan (JV) Rajendran. I began my doctoral studies in Fall 2023.

My research interests are focused on hardware security and computer architecture. Specifically, I am passionate about zero-knowledge proofs, hardware accelerators, and their intersections.
