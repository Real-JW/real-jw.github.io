About Me
Hi there! This is Jiawen's homepage.

My research interests fall into the fields of cryptography and computer architecture, especially hardware accelerators, hardware security, and their intersections. Especially on Zero-Knowledge Proofs Hardware.

I'm pursuing my PhD in SETH lab led by Prof. JV Rajendran at Texas A&M from 2023. Prior to that, I received my B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Tianjin University, China in 2023.

Impact Statement: I am passionate about designing efficient and secure hardware accelerators for emerging applications, such as machine learning, cryptography, and blockchain. And make the world a better place.
